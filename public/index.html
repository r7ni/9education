Empowering Lives With Education

Education is not only a tool it is a human right and the means to growth and a stable life. Yet, for many experiencing various forms of hardship, access to modern education is often out of reach. Studies show that lack of education is both a cause and consequence of homelessness. Without a diploma, stable employment and housing become harder to achieve.

Our mission is simple:

To provide accessible education for everyone, especially those facing barriers.

Learning should never be limited by financial struggles. Education is for everyone.

Our Initiative

With a staunch belief of "Ye are all fruits of one tree, the leaves of one branch, the flowers of one garden." We firmly believe that the progress of our community can only be achieved once all of us progress. By bridging the gap between educational institutions and those in need of support, we hope to position ourselves as the middle man. Providing those in need with certified education and prerequisite courses to help them on their educational path.

We aim to collaborate with universities and collages to provide an easy path for those in need. To maintain a high standard of education, we offer volunteer TA opportunities open to students and professionals wishing to enrich their knowledge.

Ottawa's Crisis

Financial stability remains a pressing issue in Ottawa:

  • Nearly 3,000 people were counted as homeless in Ottawa’s 2024 Point-in-Time survey.
  • Around 9,000 people used Ottawa’s emergency shelters in 2023.
  • Only 28% of homeless respondents had completed high school, and just 17% had any post-secondary education.
  • Among homeless youth, between 63% and 90% did not graduate high school.
  • Lower educational ceilings is a cause and a consequence of homelessness, limiting job opportunities and income stability.
